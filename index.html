Destination Thailand Visa (DTV)

Thailand's brand new visa designed for digital nomads, remote workers, freelancers, and long-stay travellers who want to live and work from Thailand.

  • 180 days per entry (extendable)
  • Valid for 5 years
  • Multiple entry permitted
  • For remote workers, freelancers & creatives
  • Soft power activities covered (Muay Thai, cooking classes, etc.)
  • No work permit required for foreign-sourced income
